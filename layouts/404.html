Ingredients

  • 1 dinglebop
  • 1 bunch of schleem
  • 1 grumbo
  • 1 fleeb in its juice
  • 1 Shlami
  • 2 Blamphs

Directions

  1. take the dinglebop and smooth it out with a bunch of schleem; save the schleem for later batches
  2. push the dinglebop through the grumbo and rub the fleeb against it; it's important that the fleeb is rubbed, because the fleeb has all of the fleeb juice
  3. then a Shlami shows up and he rubs it, and spits on it
  4. cut the fleeb; there are several hizzards in the way
  5. the Blamphs rub against the chumbles, then shave away the plubus and grumbo
  6. that leaves you with a regular old plumbus
